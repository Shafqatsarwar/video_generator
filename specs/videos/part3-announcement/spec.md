## 2. Creative Brief

### 2.1 Core Message

> **"If Part 1 and 2 are for the coders, Part 3 is for the builders of the modern enterprise."**

Part 3 transforms **domain experts** (CFOs, lawyers, compliance officers, HR directors) into **AI agent architects** — without requiring them to become coders.

### 2.2 Key Themes

1. **Enterprise Focus** — 16 chapters covering real business workflows
2. **Domain Expertise First** — Your 20+ years of judgment is the scarce resource
3. **Production-Ready** — Not prototypes, but validated agent configurations
4. **Governance & Safety** — AI executes, professionals judge
5. **Platform Agnostic** — Works with any AI tool (Claude, Gemini, Copilot, etc.)

### 2.3 Tone & Style

- **Professional** yet **empowering**
- **Authoritative** but **accessible**
- **Enterprise-grade** production quality
- **Inspiring** call to action
