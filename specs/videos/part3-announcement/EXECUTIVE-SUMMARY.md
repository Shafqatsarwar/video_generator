## ✅ Quality Checklist

Before launch, verify:

- [ ] Resolution is 1080p (1920x1080)
- [ ] Frame rate is 30 FPS
- [ ] Bitrate is 5 Mbps (H.264)
- [ ] Audio is clear and normalized (-16 LUFS)
- [ ] Captions are accurate and synced (SRT)
- [ ] Color contrast meets 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- [ ] Text size is 18px+ for captions
- [ ] All links work with UTM tracking
- [ ] Brand colors used correctly (#2563eb primary)
- [ ] Logo visible throughout (top-left, 24px height)
- [ ] CTA is clear and compelling
- [ ] Video plays on all devices (desktop, mobile, tablet)
