## 🎬 Video Concept

### Core Message

> **"If Part 1 and 2 are for the coders, Part 3 is for the builders of the modern enterprise."**

### Key Points (6)

1. **Enterprise Focus** — 16 chapters across 7 business domains
2. **SKILL.md Libraries** — Translate expertise into executable knowledge
3. **Production-Ready** — Validated agent configurations, not prototypes
4. **Governance Framework** — AI executes, professionals judge
5. **Platform Agnostic** — Works with Claude, Gemini, Copilot, any AI tool
6. **Real Enterprise Systems** — ERP, CRM, BI integration via MCP connectors

### Video Structure

```
0:00-0:15  → Hook: "What should you build, and why?"
0:15-0:30  → Enterprise Focus (7 domains grid)
0:30-0:45  → Domain Expertise (SKILL.md demo)
0:45-1:00  → Production-Ready (deployment diagrams)
1:00-1:15  → Governance (AI → human escalation)
1:15-1:30  → Platform Agnostic (all AI tools)
1:30-2:00  → CTA: "Start building at agentfactory.panaversity.org"
```
